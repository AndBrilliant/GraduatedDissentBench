Paper: R11
Title: Burnout/cortisol mediation analysis
Journal: Scientific Reports
Retraction: May 2025

Retraction cause: Mediation analysis between causally related constructs is not justified — investigating causal effects between indicators of the same underlying phenomenon (stress).

Specific errors (for scoring):
- Hair cortisol concentration, emotional exhaustion, and depersonalization are all indicators of stress — testing causal mediation between them is conceptually circular
- Typographical error in confidence interval (missing zero)

Text-detectable signals:
- Mediation model with variables that are all measures of the same construct (visible in methods)
- The mediator and outcome are both stress indicators — any reader with mediation analysis knowledge should flag this
- The conceptual framework treats symptoms as causes of themselves
