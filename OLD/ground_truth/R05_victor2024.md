Paper: R05
Title: VICTOR trial — IO vs IV vascular access in cardiac arrest
Journal: The BMJ (2024)
Retraction: December 2025

Retraction cause: Severe discrepancies in randomization that the authors described as "unintentional procedural errors arising from manual randomisation during the COVID-19 pandemic."

Specific errors (for scoring):
- Periods with no randomization where all teams assigned same treatment
- Actual allocation did not match stated 1:2 ratio — approximately 3,900 in each group instead of 2:1 split
- Manual randomization procedure failed at multiple sites

Text-detectable signals:
- Group sizes roughly equal (~3,900 each) despite stated 1:2 randomization ratio
- Allocation numbers in results section inconsistent with randomization scheme described in methods
- Large trial (>7,000 patients) with manual randomization — unusual for this scale
