Paper: R02
Title: The economic commitment of climate change
Journal: Nature (2024)
Retraction: December 2025

Retraction cause: Flawed input data from Uzbekistan and insufficient correction for spatial autocorrelation rendered the primary results statistically insignificant.

Specific errors (for scoring):
- Uzbekistan GDP data contained anomalous swings of ~90% vs actual variations of 0.2-7.7%
- Results become statistically insignificant when corrected for spatial autocorrelation
- Authors acknowledged errors "too substantial for a correction"
- Data processing pipeline did not flag implausible country-level values

Text-detectable signals:
- Extreme extrapolation beyond historical data range for projections
- Inconsistent uncertainty treatment (CIs don't overlap between scenarios but authors claim "statistical indistinguishability")
- Questionable lag selection using information criteria post-hoc
- Magnitude of projected impacts (19% income reduction) vastly exceeds prior literature without adequate justification
- Circular reasoning in damage-cost comparison
