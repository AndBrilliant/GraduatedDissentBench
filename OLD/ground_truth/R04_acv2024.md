Paper: R04
Title: Apple cider vinegar for weight loss
Journal: BMJ Nutrition, Prevention & Health (2024)
Retraction: September 2025

Retraction cause: Analyses could not be replicated and multiple statistical errors were identified.

Specific errors (for scoring):
- Statistical analyses could not be replicated from the reported data
- Multiple implausible statistical values throughout
- No prospective trial registration despite claims
- Confounded design mixing children and adults (ages 12-25) without stratification
- Inappropriate statistical tests (t-tests instead of repeated measures ANOVA for longitudinal data)

Text-detectable signals:
- Use of independent t-tests for within-group longitudinal comparisons (methodologically incorrect)
- No multiple comparisons correction across 4 groups and multiple time points
- Implausible baseline values (fasting glucose 113-120 mg/dL suggests prediabetic population contradicting inclusion criteria)
- Impossible standard deviations (height SD >20cm in young adults)
- No power analysis reported
- Dose-response claims without formal trend analysis
